# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 24, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **Neon Drift** ("the App", "we", "us", "our") handles information when you use our mobile game. By installing or using Neon Drift, you agree to this policy.

## 1. Who we are

Neon Drift is a free-to-play arcade game developed by an independent developer. The App is distributed through the Google Play Store.

If you have questions, contact: **contact@herdoydev.com**

## 2. Information we collect

**We do not ask you to create an account.** We do not knowingly collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifying information directly from you.

The App stores the following **locally on your device only** (via MMKV storage). This data never leaves your device through our systems:

- Your coin balance
- Your remaining lives and regeneration timestamps
- Your highest score
- Your daily bonus claim timestamp

This data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App.

## 3. Information collected by third-party advertising (Google AdMob)

Neon Drift displays advertisements served by **Google AdMob** (a service operated by Google LLC). To serve relevant advertisements and measure their performance, AdMob may collect:

- Your device's advertising identifier (AAID on Android)
- Approximate location based on IP address
- Device information (model, OS version, language)
- App usage events related to advertisements (impressions, clicks, completions)

Google's use of this information is governed by the Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can learn how AdMob uses data here:
https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

## 4. How information is used

Information collected by AdMob is used to:

- Serve advertisements (including personalized advertisements where permitted)
- Measure ad performance and frequency
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell or rent any information. We do not use your data for purposes outside of the advertising system described here.

## 5. Children's privacy

**Neon Drift is intended for users aged 13 and older.** The App is not directed at children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the App, please contact us at the address above and we will work with you and Google to address the issue.

Our AdMob configuration sets `maxAdContentRating` to **Teen (T)** and declares the App as **not directed at children**, in accordance with COPPA (United States) and GDPR-K (European Union).

## 6. Your choices and rights

### Opt out of personalized advertising (Android)

1. Open **Settings** on your Android device
2. Tap **Google** → **Ads**
3. Toggle **Delete advertising ID** or **Opt out of Ads Personalization**

After opting out, you will still see advertisements, but they will not be tailored to your interests.

### GDPR rights (European Economic Area, UK, Switzerland)

If you reside in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data processed about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Restrict or object to processing
- Request data portability
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Because we do not collect personally identifying information directly, most requests should be directed to Google, the ad provider. You can manage Google ad personalization here:
https://adssettings.google.com

### CCPA rights (California residents)

California residents have the right to request disclosure of information collected, request deletion, and opt out of the "sale" of personal information. Neon Drift does not sell personal information. For AdMob-related requests, see Google's CCPA resources:
https://privacy.google.com/businesses/compliance

## 7. Data retention

Local device data (coins, lives, high score) is retained on your device until you uninstall Neon Drift or clear its storage.

AdMob data retention is governed by Google's policies.

## 8. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited data the App processes. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Ad-network data is protected according to Google's security standards.

## 9. International users

Neon Drift is available globally. By using the App, you understand that your data may be processed in countries where Google operates advertising infrastructure, which may have data protection laws different from your own.

## 10. Third-party links

The App may display advertisements that link to third-party websites or apps. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their privacy policies before providing any information.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in the App's Google Play Store listing and in this document with a new "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes indicates your acceptance.
